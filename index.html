Next Run:
San Diego 2020

03-05 April 2020 - San Diego city CA

Run with us!

Included with all trips!

Accommodations!

Two nights in a double room not far from the city’s major attractions. We’ll also provide a city guide and recommendations for things to get into while you’re there.

The Run!

A group 5K/10K suitable for all paces through an iconic area of the city (or coast), with an additional path guide for those that want more mileage or additional runs.

The Meals!

There’s no better way to start a trip then with good food. You’ll alway get a welcome dinner on our trips and a post-run breakfast on day 2. Everything else is covered by you.

Yoga! (put this one last)

Bonus: We end each run with a yoga session tailored to runners.

Goodie Bags!

As with most 5Ks and 10K, you’ll receive a commerative t-shirt and medal on every trip. Collect them all!

Evening Social

A social activity on night two, usually a night on the town (think: comedy shows).

Conference Schedule

First Day 03 April 2020 Second Day 04 April 2020 Third Day 05 April 2020

Arrival & Check In

Meet us at our accommodations to check-in and unload. You'll unwind and meet some of our other travellers once we meet in the lobby to head to dinner. Transportation will be provided to and from dinner for those that want to return immediately after.

4:30 - 8 pm

We will provide city-guides to all guests prior to dinner for you to make out plans that work for your interests.
